## What converts (append in Phase 4)

Log each verdict as: date, note topic or format, impressions, profile visits,
free subs, paid subs, verdict (subscriber-driving / vanity), one-line takeaway.

Verified from all_time stats on 2026-06-05. Three formats drive nearly all
free-subscriber conversion. Impressions alone convert no one.

- **Community invite** ("I'd love to connect, let's chat", feature replies):
  3,136 impressions, 29 free subs. The single biggest converter. Run one
  roughly every 2 weeks, not more (novelty decays).
- **Hot take + named framework + deep-dive link** (the MRE / SRE note): 1,794
  impressions, 27 free subs. Structure: bold claim, three short contrast lines,
  name the discipline, one key insight, link. This is the workhorse.
- **Crisp single concept** (the "harness defaults" note): 126 impressions, 4
  free subs. Best per-impression rate. Cheap to produce, reliably converts.

Vanity (high reach, ~0 subs): relatable complaints ("Am I the only one moving
Openclaw to Claude Code", 198 impressions, 0 subs), empty image-only notes,
generic "use AI for free" posts. Do not repeat these formats.
