    title: 'Beyond the Private Chatbot: Bringing AI to Collaborative Work'
    abstract: |
      AI has reshaped individual productivity. We write, research, code,
      and ramp up faster than ever. But collaborative workflows haven't
      kept pace. Teams still run on pre-AI processes while each person
      builds richer context inside their own private chatbot. The result
      is a paradox: individuals get more capable as shared context grows
      more fragmented.

      This talk explores the practical patterns for building collaborative
      AI systems through shared skills, context design, and team-level
      workflow changes that make AI useful for the whole organization,
      not just one person.

Ananya Shah is an AI strategist and consultant working on the team-level
patterns that turn AI from a personal productivity tool into shared
organizational capability.
