Ships as REPORT_TEMPLATE.md in the [Field Challenge 01 repo](/01). The
template covers links to the fork and committed predictions, where AI was
used versus deterministic code, the result table (routing accuracy,
high-risk recall and precision, evidence coverage and validity, runtime,
cost), the approach, an FDE decision log with tradeoffs, a failure table
with root causes, the most interesting failure, and what would change in v2.
