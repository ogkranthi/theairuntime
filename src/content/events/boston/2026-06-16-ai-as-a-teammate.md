A full room for two talks on making AI a genuine teammate. Nirmal Jingar
(Wayfair) walked through the operating model for embedding AI in daily
engineering work, and Ananya Shah (Cartesian) made the case that collaborative
AI, shared skills and a live context layer, is the next frontier after the
private chatbot. Pizza, a Boston skyline, and a packed room of practitioners.
