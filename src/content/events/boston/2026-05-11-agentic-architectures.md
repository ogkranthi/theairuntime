Our first night was two practitioners on the same hard problem from opposite
ends: getting AI agents to actually work inside real systems.

Ray Liao walked through what broke when Inkbox let agents register themselves,
and why authentication built for humans falls apart the moment a non-human
shows up first. Michael R. Schulte then put the demo-to-production gap on
screen, deploying an agent into an existing codebase live rather than a clean
starter project, so the failure points had nowhere to hide.
